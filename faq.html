Frequently Asked Questions

List of questions

HOW DOES THE REMITTANCE SERVICE WORK?

The sender provides the full details of the beneficiary. The amount is deposited from the sender’s end where we make it available for beneficiary to collect it at Nepal through Cash Pickup or Bank Deposit.

IS THERE A FEE BRACKET DEPENDING ON THE AMOUNT OF MONEY SEND?

Flat fee of AUD 10 is applicable per transaction

THE TIME TAKEN TO TRANSFER THE MONEY?

Please allow 1- 3 business days for both the delivery methods.

DO I HAVE TO PROVIDE DETAILS OF THE RECEIVER EVERY TIME I WANT TO SEND?

When you register to our site as a sender, your details will be saved on to the system. You can log into the account next time and conduct the remittance transaction to send it to a regular who’s details would have been saved already or create a new beneficiary.

WHAT IS THE LIMIT OF THE AMOUNT PER TRANSACTION?

Due to the security and restrictions policy involved, the limit amount per transaction is AUD 10000.
