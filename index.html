How to Recieve Money

Method 1 : Cash

Collect your cash from our nearest store. Beneficiary is required to bring their id to collect the money.

Method 2: Bank Deposit

Your money is deposited into the bank. You are required to provide the details of the bank that you will deposit your money.

Method 3: NetBank Transfer

We will transfer your money into your bank account as provided by you. Incase absence of netbank in some countries the alternative way would be bank deposit.
