Services

Our services / What we can do

With NME any one can send and receive money from different parts of Nepal and worldwide at competitive charges.

Cash to cash

It’s simple and convenient!  The sender needs to visit NME payout agents or its own branch, fill the form and handover it to the agent/staff including his/her valid photo Identity Card along with Cash. The agent/staff does the transaction and provides NME Control Number. The sender communicates the Control Number to the receiver. In the similar process the receiver receives the money.

Cash to account

Beneficiary doesn’t need to collect money! Funds can be simply transferred straight to the beneficiary’s bank account and can be withdrawn at an ATM or used at a shopping outlet. For this, sender needs to visit any NME payout agents, fills the form and handover it to the agent/staff including his/her valid photo Identity Card and Cash. The agent/staff does the transaction and the money will be credited to the beneficiary account on real time. The receiver will get the notification through telephone or SMS that the remitted money had been credited to his/her account.

Cargo Facility

Our management team is dynamic and thirsty with multiple business. We provide Import and Export Services via our trade name "Royal Nepal Import & Export " duly registered in Australia. Whatever your shipment is Envelope or heavy weight, we provide you total international transportation service. we help our clients to become more profitable by providing them each and every freight requirement in a single office with costs effective price.

Import Service

We handle your import shipment quickly and efficiently is even more important. After receiving your complete sets of documents we forward it to our agent to sea import or airport for your air import shipment then provide you the status of the shipment. As soon as we clear your valued shipment from custom we will deliver to your location.

Export Service

Royal Nepal Import & Export Services based in Australia offering competitive rates is only one aspect of export forwarding services. Knowing that your shipment has been handled quickly and efficiently is even more important.
